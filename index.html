Beautiful, natural, wedding and portrait photography

Capturing all the emotion, laughter and fun

Hello. My name is Aled and I'm a South Wales Wedding Photographer. I'm based in Cardiff, in South Wales, but I also love to travel, and I have photographed weddings all over Wales and the rest of the UK. My speciality is wedding photography, and family portraits with a quirky, off-beat, alternative flavour. I have a documentary approach to wedding photography, and I capture natural, candid moments as they happen, rather than interfering to create artificial scenarios. On the day you will hardly notice that I'm there, allowing you and your guests to get on with the important business of celebrating your day. Nothing too fussy, nothing too posed. Have a good look around and if you would like to meet up for a chat to discuss your wedding or family portrait session, then please get in touch.
